Hi there!

I am an AI researcher at a decent hospital in China, Guangdong Cardiovascular institute (Top 3-4 in cardiovascular subject in China) of Guangdong Provincal People's Hospital (Top 27 in Fudan ranking). I work at the Laboratory of Artificial Intelligence and 3D Technologies for Cardiovascular Diseases as a in-lab director. The lab director is Jian Zhuang, previous dean of Guangdong Provincal People's Hospital.

I was a post-doc researcher at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of University of Notre Dame with Prof. Yiyu Shi from 2016 to 2019.
I received my Ph.D. degree and B.S. degree from the Department of Electronic Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology 2011 and 2016, respectively.

My research interests include deep learning, and medical image processing. I have several papers about hardware acceleration and image segmentation on top conferences including CVPR, AAAI, DAC, ICCAD, and top-journals including TPAMI, TCAD, TBioCAS, Nature Electronics.

Our team focuses on applying deep learning to clinic applications of cardiovascular diseases. We are collecting a massive of data from our hospital for research. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in this topic. My mail box is xiao.wei.xu@foxmail.com, and my wechat account is Tophust.
